Problem Description

Each line of input is made up of characters and 1/2 digits.
A 2-digit number can be extracted from every line.
Example:
    pqr3stu8vwx -> 38
    treb7uchet  -> 77
    a1b2c3d4e5f -> 15 (*)


Part 1

Find the sum of these numbers.


Part 2

A digit could be written with a word...
Example:
    two1nine      -> 29
    eightwothree  -> 83
    7pqrstsixteen -> 76
Now do the same as for Part 1.
